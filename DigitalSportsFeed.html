Who is Digital Sports Feed

Digital sports feed is a analytics platform which can track the performance of athletes. This platform can be used in various sports and is not limited to a single sport. This platform can do all the tracking of the data and can help the coaches to train their athletes in a way which was not possible before. What I had to do was to come up with a branding style to express the brand qualities. The feel of the brand was to look aggressive and always in motion. It should also feel intimidating.


Challenges

- Make the branding aggressive.
- Come up with appropriate typography and color.
- Create a sense of motion throughout the Brand.










Icon

The client also wantedd me to come up with two icon’s to represent a man and a women but should have the DNA of the brand.
This was to implement in the app and website because male and female will have diffrent account types and to represent this I had to come up with two icon’s.


Inspiration

The Idea was to implement the ON/OFF symbol into the logo which is represented by 0 and 1 but because Enctri fixes broken electronics.So the idea was to also break the O and 1 creating a logo.To achieve this I had looked into many different variaions and this one looked to tick all the boxes being memorable and simple at the same time.
